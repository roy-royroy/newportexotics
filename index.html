Good To Know

POLICIES

Driver Return Ride

Always paid by client or broker — Uber/Lyft back to 2136 Newport Blvd, Costa Mesa.

After-Hours Delivery

Deliveries between 8 PM – 8 AM incur an additional $25 fee to the driver.

Multiple-Car Deliveries

Each vehicle is charged separately at the full delivery rate.

Wait Time

$1.50 per minute after a 15-minute delay at the drop-off location.

Enclosed Trailer

For exotic deliveries over 50 miles — additional $150 – $250 flat rate.

Airport Drop-offs

LAX, BUR, SNA — additional $25 parking and coordination fee.

Brokering Partners

Must pay full driver rate and driver's return fare. No exceptions.

Insurance Required

Full coverage auto insurance required. Supplemental coverage options available on request.

Minimum Age

Must be 25+ years old with a valid driver's license to rent.
